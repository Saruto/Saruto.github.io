It's-a me!

I'm Andrew! I'm a software developer currently working as a UI/UX software engineer at Arriver.

I'm also a game developer, and have made a bunch of stuff using Unity! I spend most of my free time playing games, reading about game design, or, you know, making games!

brb, bio(graphy)

I graduated from the University of Michigan back in 2018 with a Bachelors in Computer Science and Mechanical Engineering. I started making games in Unity since 2014, and my best languages are C# and C++.

Onwards and (hopefully) Upwards!

I'm also the co-founder of Cuppa Games, a recently-formed game development company (we plan to announce our first major project soon, so stay tuned!). Software and video game development is going to be a major part of my life forever - I hope this little website can showcase a fraction of all the love and passion I have towards them!
